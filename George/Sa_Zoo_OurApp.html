About the SA Zoo App

The new SA Zoo app is a great way to discover the various attractions around the zoo it also highlights The Social Distance Route we have in place as well as detailing the location of all hand sanitisers and highlighting the other safety measures we have introduced.

Key Features of The SA Zoo App Include:

  1. The chance to explore the map, see your location and navigate to find your favourite animals.
  2. Read more and learn interesting facts about the species you see.
  3. Listen to audio talks from the Animal Care Team as they tell you more about selected species and the animals they care for.
  4. Look up your nearest food outlet, toilet facilities and play areas to make the most out of your journey around the zoo.
  5. Keep up to date on offers available at the zoo.
  6. Enable push notifications to receive safety reminders and updates from the zoo on the day of your visit.
  7. Important information and FAQs are also available to read during your visit should you have any queries.
