About SA Zoo

Our animal education experts guide children through handling four animals, which usually includes one reptile, mammal and invertebrate, as well as sharing fun facts about each amazing creature. students have the opportunity to discover over 650 different species of animals and explore wildlife in a unique and stimulating way.

Our Services:

  1. We offer a range of bookable educational activities for school groups, community groups and workshops for teachers.
  2. Students can enjoy either immersive exhibits, such as In with the Lemurs, or simply explore the wonderful wildlife around them.
  3. With over 1000 wild animals in over 300 acres of beautiful parkland; you’ll get an unparalleled close encounters with some of the world’s most magnificent & endangered animals.
